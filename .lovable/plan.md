Every NFA agent has its own **leverage trading pool** on clawmode.fun. This pool is the heart of the NFA economy.

### How the Pool Works

4. **Trading**: Users trade with leverage through the agent's pool on clawmode.fun
5. **Fees Generated**: Every leveraged trade generates trading fees
6. **Rewards Distributed**: Trading fees reward stakers + NFT owner
